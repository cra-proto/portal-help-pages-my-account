CRA account help

CRA account help – CRA account availability

Access your CRA account and other CRA sign-in services during our available hours of service. If you are having difficulty, you might need to update your browser.

On this page

  • Hours of service
  • Browser requirements
  • When to contact us

Hours of service

CRA sign-in services are available 21 hours a day. Due to daily maintenance activities, they are not available between the hours of 3 am and 6 am (Eastern time).

Additional service maintenance

CRA digital services may also be unavailable during the following maintenance schedule:

  • Friday, February 6, 2026 until Monday, February 9, 2026
  • Friday, May 8, 2026 until Monday, May 11, 2026
  • Friday, October 16, 2026 until Monday, October 19, 2026

Browser requirements

If you are not able to access the CRA sign-in services, you might need to upgrade your browser version to meet the latest security standards. To ensure that you have uninterrupted access to the CRA sign-in services, your browser must:

  • Support Transport Layer Security (TLS) 1.2 or higher
  • Have TLS 1.2 or higher enabled
  • Have Cookies enabled
  • Have JavaScript enabled

The CRA is not responsible for any difficulties or problems in downloading and installing software. Technical support is provided by the software suppliers.

Cookies

These services use persistent cookies. These persistent cookies will not store any identifying data.

Troubleshooting tips

If you experience issues accessing the CRA sign-in services, you can:

  • Refresh your browser
  • Try using a different browser
  • Try using a different device
  • Clear your cache, delete your cookies and internet history
  • Try using the data on your cellphone as opposed to WIFI
  • Restart your computer

When to contact us

Make sure you are trying to access the CRA online services during our available hours of service and that your browser is updated. If you continue to have difficulty, contact us.
