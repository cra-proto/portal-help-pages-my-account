Definitions and explanations

  • Welcome page
  • Log out

Welcome page

This will take you out of the secure "Change my marital status" site and back to the "My Account" welcome page.

Log out

This will take you out of the secure "Change my marital status" site.

Simultaneous Updating of Client and Spouse/Common-law Partner’s Marital Status

Individuals who are using this application to update their marital status should be aware that their spouse/common-law partner’s marital status will not be updated simultaneously. There are strict guidelines and privacy requirements surrounding another individual’s information.
