General information

You must confirm that the address and phone number information displayed is correct.

Home address

This is the address where you live. This may or may not be the same as your mailing address. It is important that we have your current home address information on file to ensure that you receive the benefits and credits that you are entitled to.

If your home address is incorrect, select No to update your home address.

Mailing address

This is the address where we send your mail. This may or may not be the same as your home address.

If your mailing address is incorrect, select No to update your mailing address.

Future address

If you have already changed your home address and your mailing address to a date in the future a message will stating the CRA has a future address on file. If you want to view this address you can use the "Change my address" link.

Phone numbers

This is the phone number information currently on your file. This may include your home and work phone numbers.

If your phone numbers is incorrect, select No to update your phone numbers.

Yes

This will take you to the next step in the application.

No

This will take you to "Update Contact Information" (BOA.ex2) page.

Welcome page

This will take you out of the secure "Apply for child benefits" site and back to the "My Account" Welcome Page.

Log out

This will take you out of the secure "Apply for child benefits" site.
