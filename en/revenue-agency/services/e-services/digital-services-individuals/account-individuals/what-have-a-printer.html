Note: On any information you provide please ensure that you write "Web Application" at the top of the page so that your application can be processed faster.

In order to complete my online application I need to provide one or more of the following:

RC65 Marital Status Form
Request a paper version of the form by calling the CRA at 1‑800‑959‑2221.

RC66SCH Status in Canada/Statement of Income Form
Request a paper version of the form by calling the CRA at 1‑800‑959‑2221.

Confirm your Marital Status Document
Provide the following information for you and your spouse or common-law partner in a letter:

  • Name;
  • Social insurance number;
  • Date of birth;
  • Marital status and effective date;
  • Mailing and home addresses; and
  • Both your signatures.

Proof of Birth
Provide an acceptable proof of birth for the requested child(ren).
