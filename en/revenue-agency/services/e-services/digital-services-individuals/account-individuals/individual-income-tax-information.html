If you need more information about your refund, or about the assessment or reassessment of a return, please consult the Notice of Assessment or Notice of Reassessment that we mailed to you.

If you still have questions about your personal taxes, you can call our Individual Income Tax Enquiries services at: 1-800-959-8281

Agents are available to assist you by phone Monday to Friday (except holidays), from 8:00 a.m. to 8:00 p.m. ET.
