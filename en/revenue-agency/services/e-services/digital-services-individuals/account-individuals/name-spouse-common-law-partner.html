General information

You must advise us of your spouse or common-law partner's given name.

Definitions and explanations

Next
Selecting "Next" will advance you to the next screen on your way to updating your new marital status.

Previous
Selecting “Previous” will take you back to your “Residency for spouse or common-law” page

Error messages

CMMS - 13   You must enter your spouse/partner's given name.
You must provide your spouse’s or common-law partner’s given name.  This is a mandatory field.

CMMS - 14   You have entered an invalid character in your spouse/partner’s given name.
Hyphens, apostrophes, periods, and spaces are allowed in the name, unless the name starts with such a character.  All other forms of punctuation are not valid.
