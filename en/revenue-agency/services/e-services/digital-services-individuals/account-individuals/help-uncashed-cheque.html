The Uncashed cheques page in My Account shows cheques sent to you by the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) that you have not cashed and are over 6 months old, dating back to 1998.

In this section:

  • Requesting a duplicate payment
  • Direct deposit
  • Display options on the uncashed cheque list
  • Explanation of cheque list headings
  • Payment acronyms

Requesting a duplicate payment

If your uncashed CRA cheque was lost or destroyed, you can ask the CRA to replace it. See "Instructions for requesting duplicate payments" on the Uncashed cheques page.

Before requesting a duplicate payment, verify both of the following:

  • Make sure your address is up to date with the CRA. To update or confirm your address, sign into My Account and select your personal profile.
  • Payee name, address, signature and date are included and correct on your completed Form PWGSC 535, Undertaking and Indemnity. Have your witness sign and date the form and enter their name and address under the signature. Your witness cannot be a family member.

After a payment is cashed or cancelled, the CRA will remove it from the uncashed cheque list on My Account within 21 days.
Find out how long it should take us to process your request for a duplicate payment using the check CRA processing times tool.

Direct deposit

The CRA sends duplicate payments by either cheque or direct deposit. We will automatically issue this payment to you by direct deposit if you are already registered.

If you have an original uncashed cheque still in your possession, but want to receive your payment through direct deposit instead, send the old cheque(s) with your request to:

Imaging and Receiver General Operations Directorate
Returned cheques
P.O. Box 2000
Matane QC  G4W 4N5

Display options on the uncashed cheque list

Use the display options on the uncashed cheque list to:

  • see up to ten payments on each page by using the standard display
  • display up to 100 payments on each page
  • sort the payment display alphabetically or by amount, reference number or date

Explanation of cheque list headings

Explanation of cheque list headings
Heading Explanation
Payment type What the payment was for (for example, income tax refund, Canada child benefit, or GST/HST credit) 
Reference number Payment reference number on the original cheque
Payment date Date of the original cheque. Duplicates will be issued with a current date.
Payment amount Amount of the original cheque, which will be the same for any duplicate payment (unless the CRA applies part or all of the payment to an existing debt)
Generate 535 For requesting a duplicate payment with Form PWGSC 535.

Uncashed cheques related to the following benefits are not listed in My Account:

  • Canada Recovery Sickness Benefit (CRSB)
  • Canada Recovery Caregiver Benefit (CRCB)
  • Canada Recovery Benefit (CRB)
  • Canada Emergency Response Benefit (CERB)
  • Canada Worker Lockdown Benefit (CWLB)
