General information

This page displays the most current address and phone number information on your file. You may want to print this page for your records.

Definitions and explanations

  • Home address
  • Mailing address
    • where we mail your Notice of Assessment
    • where we mail your instalment notices
  • Return mail
  • Phone number(s)
  • Log out

Home address

This is the address where you live. This may or may not be the same as your mailing address. It is important that we have your current home address information on file to ensure that you receive the benefits and credits that you are entitled to.

To update your home address, select Update Home Address to begin your change.

  • You will be able to change your home address to a date in the past as long as it is within the past 12 months.
  • You will be able to change your home address and your mailing address to a date in the future as long as it is within 6 months. We recommend that you print your home address change before you submit it to us.

Mailing address

This is the address where we send your mail. This may or may not be the same as your home address.

To update your mailing address, select Update Mailing Address to begin your change.

Your mailing address may also include your Notice of Assessment address or your Instalment notices address.

The Notice of Assessment address is where we mailed your [yyyy] income tax assessment.

This address appears if you have already asked us to send your Notice of Assessment and/or tax refund to an address other than your regular mailing address.

We send you a Notice of Assessment after we process your tax return. This Notice explains any corrections we made to your return, and it also tells you about any refund or any balance owing.

You cannot use this online service to change the mailing address for your next Notice of Assessment. Instead, follow these instructions:

  • If you file your return electronically, include Form T183, Information Return for Electronic Filing of an Individual's Income Tax and Benefit Return.
  • If you file your return on paper, include Form T1132, Alternative Address Authorization.

Your authorization will be in effect for one year only. You must file Form T183 or Form T1132 each time you file an income tax return. For more information, contact your local tax services office.

Your instalment notices address is where we mailed your instalment notices

This address appears if you have already asked that we send your instalment notices to an address other than your regular mailing address.

An "instalment notice" is a statement that shows the regular payments some people make to the CRA during the year. These payments cover the tax that the person would otherwise have to pay on April 30 of the following year.

You cannot use this online service to change the mailing address for your instalment notices.

For more information, see Paying Your Income Tax by Instalments on the CRA Web site or contact your local tax services office.

Return mail

We had sent you correspondence which Canada Post returned to us.

  • If your address has changed, enter your new address and select the "Next" button.
  • If your address has not changed, select "Address above is correct".

Phone number(s)

This is the phone number information currently on your file. This may include your home, work, cell, teletypewriter and pager numbers, as well as your fax numbers.

To update your phone number(s), select Update Phone number(s) to begin your change.

Log out

This will take you out of the secure "Change my address" site.
