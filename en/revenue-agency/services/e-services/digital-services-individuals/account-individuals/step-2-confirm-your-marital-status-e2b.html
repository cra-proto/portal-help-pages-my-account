General information

On this page you need to enter the date your current marital status began.

Definitions and explanations

  • Continue
  • Cancel
  • Welcome
  • Log out

Continue

This will take you to the third step in the application.

Cancel

This will take you out of the secure "Apply for child benefits" site and back to the "My Account" Welcome Page.

Welcome page

This will take you out of the secure "Apply for child benefits" site and back to the "My Account" Welcome Page.

Log out

This will take you out of the secure "Apply for child benefits" site.
