General information

This page displays your proposed new marital status.

Definitions and explanations

Cancel
"Cancel" will take you back to the "Change marital status" page. No new marital status information will be updated.

Previous
If the new marital status or the effective date is incorrect, select "Previous" to go back to the "Change marital status" page and correct your new marital status information.

Submit
By selecting “Submit” you acknowledge that all information on the screen is accurate. Your new marital status information will be updated to CRA's systems.
