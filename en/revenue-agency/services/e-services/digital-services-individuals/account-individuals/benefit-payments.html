If you need more information about your benefit or credit, check your latest notice of determination or notice of redetermination.

If you still have questions about the Canada child benefit or the GST/HST credit, call 1-800-387-1193.

Agents are available to assist you by phone Monday to Friday (except holidays), from 8 am to 8 pm, year round.
