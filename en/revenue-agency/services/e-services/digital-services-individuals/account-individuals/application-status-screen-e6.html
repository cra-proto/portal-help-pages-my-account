General information

This page displays the status of your application. Your application has been completed successfully and you will receive a payment, notice or explanation within 80 calendar days.

Definitions and explanations

  • Arrange direct deposit
  • Benefit payments
  • Welcome
  • Log out

Arrange direct deposit

This will take you out of the secure "Apply for child benefits" site and take you to the secure "Arrange my direct deposit" site.

Benefit payments

This will take you out of the secure "Apply for child benefits" site and take you to the secure "Benefit payments" site.

Welcome page

This will take you out of the secure "Apply for child benefits" site and back to the "My Account" Welcome Page.

Log out

This will take you out of the secure "Apply for child benefits" site.
