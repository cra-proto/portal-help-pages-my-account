General information

This page explains that to update your contact information you must use the "Change my address" service and then return to the "Apply for child benefits" service to start your application again.

Previous/Cancel

This will take you to the previous or cancel screen.

Update

This will take you from the “Apply for child benefits” service to the “Change my address” service.
